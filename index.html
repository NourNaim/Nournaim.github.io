WHO AM I? 

" I'm a Lebanese hybrid Architect/ designer. Currently located in Milan, using design as a lens to satisfy my never ending curiousity and earnest longing to live, learn, and create."         

Being a hybrid designer myself, belonging to both the Architecture and product design world, I have gained experiences in both fields which gave me steady motivation towards always exploring new opportunities.What truly inspires me is storytelling & urban planning.   I am highly interested in the versatile initiatives towards constantly changing communities by implementing new services and strategies because I strive to be part of the change making process. And lastly, I am interested in the ability to develop an international network, to exchange ideas and expertise in designing for a better collective future.

Animus

Experience Design & Prototyping

Interested in more details? Click here!

Set in the future, the world is technologically develo- ped and so are people’s habits. People are more en- couraged to take the time to re-evaluate their current state of mind. Technology enabled retreat centers offer users the opportunity to regain strength. All taking place in a 4 day self-discovery experience in a technology enabled re- treat center. Within a controlled and simulated environment, the user gets to experience all kinds of sensorial and sentimental aspects. Following the questions of the virtual guide, the user will reflect upon his memories of the past and realize little by little the ingredients of happiness within himself.
